What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a decentralized and secure system that prevents data tampering. It ensures transparency and trust in digital systems.

Blockchain Diagram

Why Blockchain is Important in Chemical Industry

  • Tracks chemical movement across supply chain
  • Prevents use of expired or unsafe chemicals
  • Improves transparency between manufacturers and users
  • Ensures safety and compliance with regulations

Case Study

A pharmaceutical company uses blockchain to track drug batches. Each batch is recorded on the blockchain, ensuring authenticity and preventing counterfeit drugs. This system improves safety and trust in the supply chain.
